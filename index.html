Kawaii Bento

What is Bento

A Bento is a small portion meal that is placed in a portion box. Majority of bento have the basic meat, rice or noodles, and vegetables. The art of the "kyaraben" grew in popularity when some parents would create cutesy bento for their school-aged children. This site is to help those interested in creating cute lunches that can be good for you!

How to Start

Simplest way to begin the art of bento is to buy a bento box from Amazon and putting your leftovers from the previous night!
